Joyride is a series of inspiring and soulful videos for cyclists who train indoors.

True stories of world-changing athletes, original music, gorgeous road footage, all synchronized to a coach-designed interval workout.

Launching soon.

Not long ago, cyclists training indoors needed shoot-em-up action movies or coaches yelling at them to stay motivated.

MJ and Drea, two spiritual mamas who loved riding bikes, knew there had to be a better way.

So, we made something new...

Joyride was born.

Why?

Because your life is an interval workout. And Joyride is with you.

We’re here for your everyday endurance just as much as we’re here for your monster trainer sesh. We support you though the uphill grinds and we whoo-hoo the well-earned descents. We believe deep transformation through sport is possible for every body.

Each 60-minute episode features an inspiring story and motivating original music synchronized to a challenging interval workout.

1) Download or stream the videos to your computer, TV, or mobile device• 2) Do the full 60-minute workout, or split it into two half-hour rides• 3) Easily adapt the intensity to your fitness level or structured training program
